How to get MS office for free 2021.

It is recommended to download Office via tb.rg-adguard.net/public.php, it provides unaltered ISOs of office and other windows related things. On average the the ISO of Office is around 4 GBs.

After downloading is completed, left click on the downloaded file, and then left click the setup.exe, installation will start automatically

after the installation is completed we need to activate the Office, for this head on over to GitHub to download latest version of KMS.

After KMS is downloaded open the KMS_VL_ALL_AIO folder and then click on .cmd file, if it asks admin privilges then allow it. and then press 1 to activate your MS Office Product and then press 2 to set a Auto-renewal task which will automatically renew your Product, and you won't have to worry about deactivation.

NOTE:This only works on PC. And the subscription isn't carried over to any mobile device.

NOTE: For a fully free and open source alternative to MS Office, you may also try LibreOffice.
